About Me

Tidbits about Me ...

Hi! , I'm a Nurse-turned-front-end-developer with over 8 years of experience in the healthcare industry. After discovering my passion for coding, I decided to make a career change and embarked on a journey to become a web developer. I completed a year-long internship as a DevOps engineer at NioyaTech, gaining expertise in cutting-edge technologies and collaboration.

Healthcare Skills Turned Digital Art

As I transition to the world of web design and development, I bring with me the skills of a healthcare artist: ability to see the big picture, the attention to detail, and the unwavering commitment to excellence. I am excited to use my creative palette to craft digital experiences that are not only beautiful but also intuitive and user-friendly, just like the healthcare experiences I've worked to create. I am excited to work with a team that values creativity, innovation, and collaboration. I am open to working remotely with companies from all over the world.

Unwinding in Style

In my free time, I enjoy traveling to new destinations, creating crafts with my daughter, and spending quality time with my daughter. I find inspiration in exploring the world around me and bringing that sense of adventure into my work. My creativity and imaginative spirit shine through in every project I take on, infusing my work with a sense of wonder and magic.
